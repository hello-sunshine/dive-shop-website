Welcome to Fleurieu Dive

We are the only dive shop in South Australia's Flerieu Peninsula, home to the leafy sea dragon, seahorses and seals! You can also explore with us a myriad of wrecks in Horseshoe Bay and around Granite Island.

Who we are

We're a dedicated team of divemasters with over 10 years experience guiding groups of fun divers. We have spent last five years diving around Fleurieu Peninsula and we love it!

We have founded Fleurieu Dive in 2016 to show other divers the amazing beauty of ocean life in South Australia.

Where we are

You can find us in Victor Harbor, Encounter Bay, 5 minute walk from the Bluff.
